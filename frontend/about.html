⚠️ The Problem

Students Are Prime Targets

Students seeking internships and scholarships are frequently targeted by sophisticated scammers. Common tactics include fake offer letters, WhatsApp-only communication, upfront registration fees, and impersonation of trusted brands like Google, Infosys, and NASSCOM.

Traditional security tools focus on enterprise threats. CyberShield EDU was built specifically for this overlooked, high-risk demographic.

🛡️ Our Solution

Multi-Modal AI Detection

We combine a fine-tuned NLP model, heuristic pattern matching, OCR-based image analysis, and PDF forensics into a single, unified threat intelligence platform. Every scan produces a detailed confidence score with a human-readable breakdown.

All analysis is reinforced by gamified education — turning scam awareness into an engaging, rewarding skill.

🔬 DETECTION ENGINE

8-Pillar Threat Analysis

Every scan runs through all active pillars simultaneously. Results are correlated and fused into a single tri-state verdict: Safe, Suspicious, or Scam.

🧠
PILLAR 01
NLP Text Analysis
A fine-tuned DistilBERT student model detects urgency, pressure tactics, and reward-baiting patterns in text messages and emails.
🔗
PILLAR 02
URL Heuristic Scanner
Checks for typosquatting, suspicious TLDs, free hosting domains, and IP-based URLs. Validates SSL and domain age.
🖼️
PILLAR 03
Image & OCR Forensics
Uses Tesseract OCR to extract text from uploaded images, then runs the full NLP + pattern pipeline on the extracted content.
📄
PILLAR 04
PDF Document Analysis
Extracts text from PDF offer letters and scholarship documents. Detects red flags like fee requests, vague company details, and missing legal identifiers.
🗄️
PILLAR 05
Dynamic Pattern Engine
A database-driven regex and keyword engine with 20+ active threat rules. Patterns are weighted by risk score and updatable by admins in real-time.
✅
PILLAR 06
Brand Trust Registry
Checks sender domains against a curated database of verified companies. Detects impersonation of Google, Microsoft, NASSCOM, and other trusted brands.
🔄
PILLAR 07
Correlation Engine
Fuses signals from all active pillars using a weighted scoring algorithm. Applies configurable thresholds from the database to produce the final tri-state verdict.
🎮
PILLAR 08
Gamification Engine
Awards XP and badges for scans, quiz completions, and module mastery. Tracks level progression and syncs with the user profile in real-time.
