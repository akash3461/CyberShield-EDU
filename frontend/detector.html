Understanding the Confidence Score

The AI Confidence Score represents how closely the analyzed content matches known fraudulent patterns in our database.

90% - 100%

High certainty of a scam. Features align perfectly with verified fraud tactics.

70% - 89%

Suspicious indicators found. Proceed with extreme caution.

50% - 69%

Potential risk detected. Patterns are ambiguous but warrant a second look.

Below 50%

Low matching with fraud signatures. Usually indicates safe content.

Note: Our AI evaluates linguistics, metadata, and structural patterns. Always use your best judgment in addition to our analysis.
